Welcome to ColorFightII

ColorFightII is an open source artificial intelligence platform, created by Tian Gao

In ColorFightII, you need to write a bot to expand, develop and conquer. You need to keep a good balance between war and economy, and try to collect more gold in the end of the game.

Pre-season phase 1, 07/01/2019 - 07/21/2019

In pre-season phase 1, everything in alpha stage. The server may restart anytime and the ongoing game will be deleted. Every game is ranked game to test the ladder system and the leaderboard. The game rules may update frequently to fix loopholes or to balance the stategy.
